I am a Computer Science undergraduate at the University of California, Davis, interested in agentic AI systems, information retrieval, data normalization, and practical software tools that help people make better decisions.

My current work centers on building a UC Davis AI Academic Advisor: an agentic RAG system for course planning and advising. The system combines structured course data, degree requirements, student reviews, function calling, conversation summarization, and schedule-building workflows to help students explore realistic academic plans.

Research and engineering interests
======

- Agentic RAG and tool-using AI systems
- Academic advising, educational technology, and student-facing decision support
- Data scraping, normalization, and knowledge-base construction
- Graph algorithms, route planning, and real-world systems engineering
- Human-centered product design for technical tools

Selected highlights
======

- Built an autonomous AI-powered course planning and advising system using Agentic RAG and function calling.
- Constructed a structured knowledge base from 9,000+ course records, degree prerequisites, and 10,000+ student reviews.
- Designed a context summarization module to reduce token cost while preserving multi-turn coherence.
- Engineered a C++ route planning system using OpenStreetMap data, transit networks, Dijkstra's algorithm, Makefiles, GoogleTest, and Google Mock.
- Led the Photography Team for the Chinese Union of UC Davis, coordinating event coverage and portrait photography for community activities.

I also enjoy landscape photography. This site includes a dedicated [Photography](/photography/) page for landscapes, natural light, and outdoor scenes.
